Root Destroyer

A powerful, non-toxic solution made from pure copper sulfate crystals designed to tackle invasive roots in sewer lines, drain pipes, and septic systems. Perfect for maintaining your plumbing without harming the environment or your trees.

Eliminate Invasive Roots Safely

Root Destroyer is formulated to safely kill existing roots and prevent new growth in your drains. This ensures a long-lasting solution for your waste system, keeping water flow smooth and minimizing the chances of clogs.

It is specially crafted for septic tanks, where it effectively combats root intrusion without damaging the surrounding greenery or ecosystem.

Efficient and Easy to Use

Apply directly into the toilet bowl near the sewer line or use in your septic tank with the recommended dose to achieve maximum effectiveness. With clear dosing instructions, it is easy to use for both septic and drain applications.

Perfect for homeowners and property managers looking for a reliable, chemical-free option to maintain their plumbing systems. Use it twice a year or as needed to keep your drains free from root buildup.
